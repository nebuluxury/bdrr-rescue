More Than a Shelter

How we save lives

Rescue is just the beginning. We rehabilitate, train, and stand by every dog until they are truly home.

Adopt

Find your perfect match from 1,600+ dogs, every one spayed, vaccinated, and microchipped.

Foster

Open your home short-term. From puppies to hospice, fostering saves two lives, theirs and the next dog's.

Heal

Our Miracle Fund covers life-saving surgeries and medical care for the dogs who need it most.

Serve

We train rescued dogs as service companions for veterans living with PTSD, at no cost to them.

Golden Years, Golden Hearts

It's never too late for a fresh start.

Senior dogs are the last to be adopted and the first to love you back. They are calm, house-trained, and endlessly grateful, and they wait the longest for a home.

We give every senior dog the dignity they deserve, and we help match them with people ready to make their golden years shine.
