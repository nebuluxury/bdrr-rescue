The Miracle Fund

For the dogs who need a miracle.

Some dogs arrive broken, hit by cars, riddled with heartworm, or worse. The Miracle Fund pays for the extraordinary medical care that gives them a real shot at life.

These are the cases most shelters turn away. Because we are no-kill, we don't. Your gift is often the difference between goodbye and a whole new life.
