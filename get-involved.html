There's a way for everyone to help.

You don't have to adopt to change a life. Foster, volunteer, or spread the word, every hand makes room for one more dog.

Foster

Open your home, save two lives

Fostering frees up space at the ranch for the next rescue. We cover food, supplies, and all vet care, you provide the couch and the love.

Primary Foster

A temporary home for a dog waiting to be adopted.

Medical Foster

Help a dog recover from surgery, injury, or illness.

Neonatal Foster

Bottle-feed and nurture orphaned newborn puppies.

Fospice Foster

Give a senior dog comfort and love in their final chapter.

Volunteer

Give a little time, get a lot of tail wags.

From walking and bathing dogs to photography, transport, and events, our volunteers are the heartbeat of the ranch. Ages 16+ solo, or 8+ with an adult.

Even our Ranch Readers program, where kids read aloud to shy dogs, helps them learn to trust again.
